MTurk Daily Survey Study Smartphone App Download Instructions Page

 How to download the app: [Please save this information for future use]

Step 1: Go to this website on the browser of your phone or tablet: http://tinyurl.com/downloadMTurkApp

Step 2: Note that there are slightly different instructions for Android and iOS/Apple users. Please follow the appropriate instructions to install the app.

Step 3: After the app is installed, open the app on your phone or tablet and enter your MTurk ID very carefully.

Step 4: Allow for push notifications -- this will allow the app to let you know when the survey is ready each evening at 8pm local time. You will have until midnight to complete that day's survey. You will receive your first alert on Tuesday, November 12, 2019 at 8pm.

Step 5: Check whether a data file has been created for you in our server by going to this website: http://tinyurl.com/mturkAppValidation
You will be asked to enter your MTurk ID and then the status of your installation will appear. You can always access this website at the bottom of the App Download Page.

If you do NOT receive a notification at 8pm on Tuesday, November 12th:
First, please email us to let us know (at DailySurveyStudy@gmail.com) -- this will help us learn more about the issue and solve it for the future.

Second, please set an alarm on your phone or tablet for 8pm local time each night. We apologize for this extra step and will work to fix the issue. In the meantime, we want to make sure that you can fully participate in our research and setting an alarm will help remind you about each day's survey.

Thank you for participating in our research!
